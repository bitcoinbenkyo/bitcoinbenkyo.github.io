Mina of Ankoku

暗国のミナ

The Sharp Guide · 鋭いガイド

Born on Ankoku — a hidden island off the coast of Japan. The day before her parents' retirement, a government decree erased their pension. No appeal. No warning. She escaped, but border guards seized everything: cash, jewelry, family heirlooms.

The only thing they couldn't take were the 12 words in her head — her Bitcoin seed phrase. Now she sends sats to her parents every month. She refuses to let them starve under the system.

"They called it monetary policy. I call it theft on a timer." — Mina of Ankoku

Sato-san

サトウさん

The Cheerful Guide · 明るいガイド

Raised in an army family, Sato moved country to country throughout his childhood. He witnessed corrupt governments failing their people, currencies collapsing overnight, and protest funds seized without warning.

He discovered Bitcoin as the unseizable fuel for freedom movements — money that no government can freeze, confiscate, or devalue. Warm, friendly, and endlessly patient, he teaches what he wishes he had known sooner.

"They didn't take it in one day. They took it slowly, so you wouldn't notice." — Sato-san
